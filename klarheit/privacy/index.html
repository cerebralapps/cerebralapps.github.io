INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following data types from our App:

  1. Identifiers: We collect identifiers such as UserID for app functionality, and analytics.

Please note that this information may be collected but is not linked to your identity.

We use your information for the following purposes:

  3. Customer Support: Your information helps us to provide you with customer support and handle any queries or disputes you might have.
  4. Analytics: To gain insights of the app performance and how users interact with the different features. This helps us in designing and developing a better product and help more people.

SHARING OF YOUR INFORMATION

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy or as indicated at the time of collection.

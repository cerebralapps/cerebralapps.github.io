PRIVACY POLICY FOR MONDRIAN

Effective Date: March 1, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Mondrian (the "App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information and what choices you have with respect to the information.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following data types from our App:

  1. Identifiers: We collect identifiers such as UserID to authenticate the user's identity. This information is used for App functionality, third-party advertising, and tracking purposes.
  2. Purchases: Information related to your purchases made within the App is collected to keep track of your transaction history, provide customer support, and manage any disputes. It is also used for App functionality, third-party advertising, and tracking purposes.
  3. Usage Data: We collect data on product interactions for tracking and third-party advertising purposes.

Please note that this information may be collected but is not linked to your identity.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information for the following purposes:

  1. App Functionality: We use your information to enable App features, authenticate your identity, ensure server uptime, minimize App crashes, improve scalability and performance.
  2. Security: To implement security measures and prevent fraud.
  3. Customer Support: Your information helps us to provide you with customer support and handle any queries or disputes you might have.
  4. Tracking: We use tracking to evaluate the performance of third-party ads for the Mondrian application.

TRACKING AND OPT-OUT OPTIONS

Tracking is optional. You can choose to opt-out of tracking by adjusting your preferences within the App settings. This will not affect your access to App functionality.

SHARING OF YOUR INFORMATION

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy or as indicated at the time of collection.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice (such as adding a statement to our homepage or sending you a notification).
